Homemade New York style pizza

Learn how to make an authentic New York-style pizza at home using a conventional oven!

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 500g (3 ¾ cups) bread flour
  • 325g (1 ⅓ cups) water
  • 2g (½ tsp) instant yeast
  • 10g (2 tsp) salt

Sauce:

  • 1 can (14oz / 400g) San Marzano tomatoes
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 crushed garlic clove

Toppings:

  • 150g (1 ½ cups) shredded low-moisture mozzarella
  • Olive oil (optional)

Instructions

Dough Preparation (Day Before Baking)

  1. Mix all dough ingredients in a bowl until fully combined.
  2. Knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Divide into two equal dough balls, coat lightly with oil, and refrigerate for 12–48 hours.

Sauce Preparation

  1. Blend all sauce ingredients together.
  2. Let rest for at least 30 minutes before use.

Pizza Assembly & Baking (Baking Day)

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C) with a pizza stone or steel for 45–60 minutes.
  2. Take dough out of the fridge 1 hour before baking.
  3. Stretch dough into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface.
  4. Spread ⅓–½ cup of sauce evenly, add shredded mozzarella, and drizzle olive oil (optional).
  5. Transfer to the pizza stone and bake for 6–8 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Let rest for 1–2 minutes, then slice and enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade New York-style pizza! 🍕
